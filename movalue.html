Movalue

Start-up project UI design

Type

Start-up project

Duration

08.2016 - Now

Scope

User Interface / User Experience / Interaction Design

Brief

Movalue is a start-up project I built with another friend. I was in charge of the user experience design and user interface mockup. This mockup is a first iteration, it was introduced in several pitches for stakeholders and potential investors, and was appreciated a lot. We are still looking for opportunities to develop it further at this moment.

Concept

For a lot of Chinese students who study abroad, to ship something from China to where they study is sometimes annoying. An express delivery demands a lowest fee at around 25 euros. But chances are that the items are very small and it's not cost-effective to do one delivery; or the items are urgently needed but the delivery will take at least 1 week. In this case, it becomes normal to ask people who is flying back and forwards to bring stuffs for them. While one can't always find this person in his/her acquaintance. So we offer an solution on Movalue.

Logo Design

Movalue is created on the notion of Sharing Economy. Oversea students post their items and search for travelling people on this platform. Travelling people, on the other hand, can also post their journeys and capabilities of weight, or search for suitable items to carry. The carrier will have to keep an amount of money (suggested by the sender) in their wallet before the item has reached its destination, but the carrier will also receive a delivery fee from the sender as a reward. In this way, the parcel is ensured safe, the delivery is made efficient, and every move is made more valuable for both the carrier and the sender.

An interaction flow was made to picture breifly where the app will lead users, as well as a rough hierarchy relationship.

At current state, pages for main functions were built to elaborate the user experience for the pitches and some users. The visual sytle of User Interface was also presented in these pages.
