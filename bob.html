Dr.BoB

Website design for Demand Response Ecosystem

Type

Business project

Duration

07.2016 - 08.2016

Scope

Web Design / Front-end / Interaction Design

Brief

This project was a website design finished at Duneworks, where I worked for 1.5 month in 2016. Demand Response in Blocks of Buildings (Dr. BoB) is an EU project that Duneworks participated in. Dr. BoB aims at easing the peak demand of electricity through time-based tariffs and other financial incentives, and therefore optimize grid capability. It is an enormous ecosystem where inhabits multiple stakeholders and elements. Dr.BoB felt the need for a tool to clarify the complex.

Information should be changed among the stakeholders, and it is very important to see how each one stands in and affect the ecosystem. The old system mapping is messy and flat.

Chaotic information & Layered information

After thorough understanding of the ecosystem, I categorized the elements and put them into 3 layers. The social, physical and virtual elements of Demand Response were projected on the illustration. The visualization clearly demonstrates how each of the elements connects to others in the space, and how information flows from one to another. During Dr.BoB's development, this tool aided the consortium to communicate, and it was proven to be clear and simple to understand in Dr.BoB's interviews of stakeholders in their demo sites.
