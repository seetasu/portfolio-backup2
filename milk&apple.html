Apple +
Milk

Exploring innovative milk processing

Exhibited in DDW 2014

Role in the group

Concept generation, filming & editing

Duration

09.2014

Scope

Food Design / Cultural Perception

Concept

Milk plays a pivotal role in Dutch culture. While more innovation in the milk processing and serving is expected. One’s tradition becomes another’s novelty when members from different culture backgrounds work together.
In the concept, apples (and some other vegetables) are used as the ‘bowl’, following the Chinese ethnic convention of serving food in edible containers; western milk processing reacts with eastern cooking techniques, giving rise to this renovation against orthodox milk culture.
In addition, through observing from a distance, participating as the ‘co-chef’, to actually being in charge of the dish, the experience provokes reflection on the relationship between designers and users.
